********
Glossary
********

.. glossary::

	Incid
		An *incremental identifier* used as a unique reference to a logical group of one or more features.

	TOID
		A unique *topographical identifier* applied to features in Ordnance Survey's MasterMap product.

	Fragment
		An incremental number used as a unique reference to individual subsets (fragments) of a TOID.
